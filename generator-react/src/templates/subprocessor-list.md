## Data Transfer Mechanisms

For subprocessors located outside the EEA/UK, we employ:
- **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)**: EU Commission-approved contracts
- **Adequacy Decisions**: Where the jurisdiction has been deemed adequate by the EU Commission
- **Supplementary Measures**: Additional security measures beyond SCCs where required

## Subprocessor Security Requirements

All subprocessors must meet our security standards:
- ✅ SOC 2 Type II or ISO 27001 certification (or equivalent)
- ✅ Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- ✅ Data Processing Agreement in place
- ✅ Regular security audits
- ✅ Incident response procedures
- ✅ Business continuity planning
- ✅ Subprocessor's own subprocessors approved
