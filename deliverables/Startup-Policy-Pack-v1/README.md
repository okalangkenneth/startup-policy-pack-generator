# Startup Policy Pack (GDPR, AI & Privacy)

Thanks for your purchase! This pack helps you generate five key startup policies in minutes.

1. Open \generator\dist\index.html\ in your browser.
2. Fill in company details.
3. Click **Generate ZIP**.
4. Review and publish.
